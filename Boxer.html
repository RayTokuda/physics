Analyze the Impulse-Momentum Theorem and the inverse relationship between Force and Time.

Impulse - Momentum Theorem

F t = m Δv

This theorem reveals some interesting relationships, such as the INVERSE relationship between FORCE and TIME when the change in momentum is constant.
